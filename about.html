About Our Company

Welcome to Kriz's Garage, where the magic of vintage cycling comes to life. With a passion for all things retro, we curate a collection of classic and timeless bicycles that transport you to a different era. Our goal is to share the joy of cycling's golden days, where each pedal stroke tells a story of craftsmanship and nostalgia. Join us in embracing the spirit of vintage cycling at Kriz's Garage.

Our journey began with a desire to honor the legacy of these timeless machines. We painstakingly search the corners of the world to rescue forgotten treasures, meticulously restoring each bike to its original glory. From the roar of the engine to the curve of the fender, every detail is a testament to the artistry of a bygone era.

Join us on this journey through time, where the past comes alive through the hum of the engine and the wind in your hair. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a curious novice, our doors are open to share the heritage, passion, and thrill that vintage motorcycles offer. Discover your own story as you embrace the spirit of the open road with Kriz's Garage.
